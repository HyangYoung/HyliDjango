About Hyli
Why We Started?

Hyli’s mission is to lead our clients to the pinnacle of their business performance by providing services which add value to their product. Hyli connects clients with the right certification agency, supports the application and certification process, and provides an outlet for continuous improvement. Once certified, Hyli guides clients to use the certification to break barriers into new markets.

Today’s consumers are spending their money on products that match their lifestyle, made by companies with values similar to their own. Certifications are a way for companies to display their product quality, preserved identity, vegan status, or adherence to religious requirements. Certification is a third-party verification that a product conforms to a set of standards defined by the industry. Certification logos have become commonplace on product packaging and company marketing campaigns, and trends show these logos becoming more familiar to consumers as they demand a higher standard of quality.

Whether it’s an advertisement of your product’s quality, its adherence to dietary or religious guidelines, or a statement about your company’s dedication to a social or economic cause, choosing the certification(s) that aligns with culture of your company is an important first step towards reaching your target audience.
